Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2025

1. Introduction

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which Noventra Consulting Group ("we," "us," or "our"), the operator of LetterLift ("Service"), provides refunds for subscription fees and other charges. This policy is designed to be fair to both our customers and our business while complying with applicable consumer protection laws in the United States.

By subscribing to our Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Refund Policy in conjunction with our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

2. Subscription Refund Eligibility

2.1 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscribers. If you are not satisfied with our Service for any reason, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your initial subscription date.

2.2 Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a refund under our 30-day guarantee, you must:

  • Be a first-time subscriber to LetterLift
  • Request the refund within 30 calendar days of your initial subscription
  • Have used the Service in good faith and in accordance with our Terms of Service
  • Not have violated any terms of our Terms of Service
  • Not have previously received a refund from LetterLift

2.3 Partial Month Refunds

For subscriptions canceled after the 30-day guarantee period, we do not provide prorated refunds for partial months. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle.

3. Refund Process and Timeline

3.1 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you must contact our customer support team through one of the following methods:

  • Email: support@letterliftapp.com
  • Phone: +1 (424) 325-05-98
  • Online Form: Submit a request through our contact form
  • Mail: 1230 Rosecrans Ave Suite 300, Manhattan Beach, CA 90266

3.2 Required Information

When requesting a refund, please provide:

  • Your full name and email address associated with the account
  • Your subscription plan and billing information
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Any relevant order or transaction numbers
  • The date of your initial subscription

3.3 Processing Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: We will acknowledge your refund request within 24 hours
  • Review: We will review your request within 3-5 business days
  • Decision: You will receive a decision within 7 business days
  • Processing: Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days
  • Receipt: Refunds typically appear in your account within 3-7 business days after processing

4. Refund Methods

4.1 Original Payment Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the subscription. This includes:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards
  • PayPal accounts
  • Bank transfers (where applicable)

4.2 Alternative Refund Methods

In cases where the original payment method is no longer available, we may issue refunds through:

  • Bank transfer to a verified account
  • Check mailed to your billing address
  • Store credit (with your consent)

4.3 Processing Fees

We do not charge processing fees for legitimate refund requests. However, third-party payment processors may retain their original processing fees, which are beyond our control.

5. Circumstances for Refunds

5.1 Eligible Refund Scenarios

We will consider refund requests in the following circumstances:

  • Technical issues that prevent you from using the Service
  • Service outages lasting more than 24 hours
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges
  • Unauthorized charges on your account
  • Failure to deliver promised features or functionality
  • Dissatisfaction within the 30-day guarantee period

5.2 Non-Eligible Refund Scenarios

We generally do not provide refunds in the following circumstances:

  • Change of mind after the 30-day guarantee period
  • Failure to use the Service due to personal circumstances
  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • Requests made more than 30 days after subscription
  • Abuse of the refund policy
  • Chargebacks initiated without contacting us first

5.3 Exceptional Circumstances

We may consider refunds outside our standard policy in exceptional circumstances, such as:

  • Medical emergencies or serious illness
  • Military deployment
  • Natural disasters affecting your ability to use the Service
  • Death of the subscriber

Such requests will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis and may require supporting documentation.

6. Subscription Cancellation

6.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time through:

  • Your account settings on our website
  • Contacting customer support
  • Using our cancellation form
  • Calling our support line

6.2 Cancellation Effective Date

Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will continue to have access to the Service until that date.

6.3 Reactivation

You may reactivate your subscription at any time, but previous refund eligibility periods do not reset.

7. Billing Disputes and Chargebacks

7.1 Contact Us First

Before initiating a chargeback with your bank or credit card company, please contact us directly. Most billing issues can be resolved quickly and amicably through direct communication.

7.2 Chargeback Consequences

If you initiate a chargeback without first contacting us:

  • Your account may be immediately suspended
  • You may be charged additional fees to cover chargeback costs
  • Future refund requests may be denied
  • Legal action may be taken to recover costs

7.3 Fraudulent Chargebacks

Chargebacks initiated after receiving our services without attempting to resolve the issue directly may be considered fraudulent and will be disputed accordingly.

8. Student and Educational Discounts

8.1 Student Verification

Student discounts require valid student identification. If student status cannot be verified, the discount may be revoked and additional charges may apply.

8.2 Student Refund Policy

Student subscriptions are subject to the same refund policy as regular subscriptions, with the following additions:

  • Refunds may be denied if student status was misrepresented
  • Graduation or change in student status does not qualify for refunds
  • Academic calendar changes do not affect refund eligibility

9. Business and Enterprise Refunds

9.1 Business Account Refunds

Business and Enterprise accounts may have custom refund terms outlined in their service agreements. These terms take precedence over this general policy.

9.2 Volume Discounts

Accounts receiving volume discounts may have different refund calculations based on actual usage and the discount structure.

9.3 Custom Contracts

Enterprise customers with custom contracts should refer to their specific agreement terms for refund policies.

10. Technical Issues and Service Credits

10.1 Service Level Agreement

We strive to maintain 99.9% uptime for our Service. In cases of extended outages, we may provide service credits or refunds as follows:

  • 4-24 hours downtime: 10% service credit
  • 24-48 hours downtime: 25% service credit
  • 48+ hours downtime: 50% service credit or full refund

10.2 Planned Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance periods do not qualify for service credits or refunds, provided reasonable notice is given.

10.3 Third-Party Issues

Service interruptions caused by third-party providers (internet, hosting, etc.) may not qualify for refunds unless the outage exceeds 48 hours.

11. Refund Abuse Prevention

11.1 Monitoring

We monitor refund requests for patterns of abuse, including:

  • Multiple refund requests from the same user
  • Suspicious account activity
  • Violation of Terms of Service
  • Fraudulent payment methods

11.2 Consequences of Abuse

Users who abuse our refund policy may face:

  • Denial of future refund requests
  • Account suspension or termination
  • Legal action to recover costs
  • Reporting to relevant authorities

12. State-Specific Rights

12.1 California Residents

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and other state laws. These rights do not affect your refund eligibility but provide additional consumer protections.

12.2 Other State Laws

Some states have specific consumer protection laws that may provide additional refund rights. This policy does not limit any rights you may have under applicable state law.

12.3 Federal Protections

Federal laws, including the Fair Credit Billing Act, provide additional protections for billing disputes and unauthorized charges.

13. International Customers

13.1 Currency and Exchange Rates

Refunds for international customers will be processed in the original currency. Exchange rate fluctuations may affect the final amount received.

13.2 International Banking

International refunds may take longer to process due to banking regulations and may incur additional fees from intermediary banks.

13.3 Local Laws

International customers may have additional rights under their local consumer protection laws, which this policy does not limit.

14. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices, legal requirements, or other factors. Changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications to active subscribers
  • Prominent notices on our website
  • Updates to this page with revision dates

Material changes that reduce refund eligibility will not apply to subscriptions purchased before the change takes effect.
